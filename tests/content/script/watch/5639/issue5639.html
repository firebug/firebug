Issue 5639: Allow deleting all watch expressions at once

Steps to reproduce

  1. Open Firebug
  2. Enable and switch to the Script panel
  3. Switch to the Watch side panel
  4. Add this watch expression (without quotes): "a"
  5. Add this watch expression (without quotes): "b"
  6. Right click on the "a" watch expression and click on Delete All Watches
    ⇒ Every watch expression should be removed.
  7. Redo steps 4 and 5
  8. Right click somewhere else inside the Watch side panel and choose Delete All Watches from the context menu
    ⇒ Every watch expression should be removed.

Expected result

  • All watch expressions should be removable at once.
